Project: Sports analytics using Computer Vision

  • Detecting moving objects in video sequences captured by an aerial vehicle over a soccer field. The goal is to detect either the soccer ball or soccer players and count their occurrences on each side of the field.
  • Required materials include a personal computer, two video sequences, and Python 3.10 with libraries like OpenCV, NumPy, ArgParse, glob, and text-wrap.The methodology involves analyzing the problem requirements and developing separate scripts for the two datasets.
  • For the first dataset, the algorithm filters the image to detect the moving soccer ball.
  • For the second dataset, it utilizes background subtraction and morphology to track moving soccer players and the ball. Perimeter detection is implemented, and the user defines the field's perimeter by clicking with the mouse or trackpad.
  • The results show successful detection of objects and counting occurrences, although some noise and processing errors are noted.
